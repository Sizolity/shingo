# Wiki As Compounding Artifact

A wiki is a compounding artifact when every source and useful question can improve the durable knowledge structure.

In the LLM wiki pattern, cross-references, contradictions, summaries, and syntheses are maintained over time. New work builds on prior work because the LLM updates the wiki instead of treating each query as a fresh retrieval problem.

The compounding effect depends on filing useful answers back into the wiki and periodically linting the structure for drift.
